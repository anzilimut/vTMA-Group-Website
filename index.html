PILOT'S PARADISE

Welcome to the Skies

Trans Maldivian Airways Seaplanes welcomes you

to the paradise of pilotage.

Our mission is to provide an extraordinary flying experience

over the stunning Maldivian atolls.

As a pilot with us, you'll navigate through the endless blue,

touching down on azure waters near

some of the world's most exclusive resorts.

Our carefully maintained DHC-6 Twin Otters,

specially equipped for water takeoffs and landings,

offer an exciting challenge for seasoned pilots and a

unique opportunity for those looking to expand their flying skills.

Join our community of aviators and discover the true beauty of Maldivian aviation.

TAKE THE CONTROLS

Exploring the Atolls

As a pilot with Trans Maldivian Airways Seaplanes, you'll have the incredible opportunity to explore the breathtaking Maldivian atolls. Our routes will take you through an ever-changing landscape of islands, lagoons, and crystal-clear waters. Experience the thrill of water landings and takeoffs while discovering remote airstrips near world-class resorts. Your journey with us is more than just a flight; it's a scenic adventure that will leave you in awe of the Maldives' natural beauty. Join us and embrace the challenge of navigating this tropical paradise.

A PILOT'S DELIGHT

Culinary Adventures

In between flights, indulge in culinary delights at our partner resorts. The Maldives is not only known for its stunning landscapes but also its diverse cuisine. As a pilot, you'll have the opportunity to savor fresh seafood, tropical fruits, and international dishes in beautiful settings. Enjoy the hospitality of the resorts you serve and make your flying experience even more memorable. Join Trans Maldivian Airways Seaplanes and elevate your aviation career in a place where the sky meets the sea.
