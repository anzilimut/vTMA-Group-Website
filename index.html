Welcome to the Skies

Discover paradise with Trans Maldivian Airways Seaplanes. We specialize in extraordinary flights over the breathtaking Maldivian atolls. Join us to explore this unique aviation beauty and create cherished, unforgettable memories.

TAKE THE CONTROLS

Exploring the Atolls

As a Trans Maldivian Airways Seaplanes pilot, you’ll explore the stunning Maldivian atolls. Our routes offer a diverse landscape of islands and lagoons. Experience water landings and takeoffs, and discover remote airstrips near top resorts. Your flight with us is a scenic adventure through the Maldives’ natural beauty. Join us for this tropical navigation challenge.

A PILOT'S DELIGHT

Culinary Adventures

In between flights, indulge in culinary delights at our partner resorts. The Maldives is not only known for its stunning landscapes but also its diverse cuisine. As a pilot, you'll have the opportunity to savor fresh seafood, tropical fruits, and international dishes in beautiful settings. Enjoy the hospitality of the resorts you serve and make your flying experience even more memorable. Join Trans Maldivian Airways Seaplanes and elevate your aviation career in a place where the sky meets the sea.
